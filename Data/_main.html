What is xTalk?
xTalk is a lunchtime talk initiative created by and for people like you.
Anyone can be a presenter and talks are open to everyone.
Come along in your lunch break to share inspirational ideas, discuss interesting topics and discover new things.

xTalk presentations and workshops are held at Technology One HQ in Brisbane, QLD, Australia.
(For staff at other offices around the world who cannot attend, check for your faviourite talk videos on this site.)
